Meet Our Research Team

Principal Investigator
Magesh Chandramouli

Dr. Magesh Chandramouli is a Professor of Computer Graphics Technology at Purdue University, Northwest, and a Fellow of the Software Sustainability Institute, UK. He holds a Ph.D. from Purdue University, West Lafayette, and has received recognition for his work, including the Warner Professional Practice Award and the University Outstanding Scholar Award. With expertise in Virtual Reality, Visualization, Genetic Algorithms, and Design Optimization, Dr. Chandramouli has been involved in various research projects, including NSF grants and the SMART fund. He is a member of IEEE, American Society of Engineering Education, MENSA, and Epsilon Pi Tau. Additionally, he has authored a book titled '3D Modeling & Animation: A primer' published by Taylor & Francis Publishers/CRC Press.
